Second City



Rating:11/10


When you are looking to see a great form of entertainment but don't want to drop hundreds of dollars you should check out Second City. The Second City is an improvisational comedy enterprise and is the first ongoing improvisational theater troupe to be continually based in Chicago. Be prepared to spend the night laughing!



The Bean



Rating:8/10


Would I have a Chicago review website without the Bean... no. I never really understood the hype at first, but I do recommend taking the time to stop by Millenium Park since it is such a beautiful place to walk around in the heart of the city. Get your pictures in at the bean and then head to any of the awesome restaurants across the street.
