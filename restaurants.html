LondonHouse



Rating:10/10


If you are looking for the best rooftop view of the city, this should be your next restaurant destination. Everytime I come I can't help but take a few pictures. The drinks and food compliment the amazing view and I always recommend London House to anyone new to Chicago.



Pizza Portofino



Rating:8/10


Another great restaurant is Pizza Portofino which is located on the Chicago River. It is a Chic Italian eatery with a riverfront terrace offering craft pizza & pasta, grilled seafood & wines. I recommend going during the summer and enjoying a slice of margarita pizza on the water.



The Kitchen



Rating:8/10


The Kitchen is an upscale American restaurant also located on the Chicago river. You can find local ingredients featured in modern American dishes at this rustic-chic spot with an open kitchen. The Kitchen American Bistro in River North has globally-inspired shared plates & killer drinks crafted in a stylish, casual space.
